Description

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor — try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork and ½ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 can tomato sauce and 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley and 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, shredded mozzarella
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat until browned. Add onion and cook until translucent. Stir in tomatoes, parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Boil lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until al dente.
  3. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan, eggs, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Layer the sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture. Top with mozzarella.
  5. Bake covered for 30 minutes, uncover, and bake until golden brown.
