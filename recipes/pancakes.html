Description

These classic fluffy pancakes are light, soft, and perfect for breakfast. Serve them with butter, syrup, or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat.
  4. Pour ¼ cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  5. Serve warm with syrup and butter.
