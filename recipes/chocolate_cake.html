Description

This moist chocolate cake is rich, fluffy, and perfect for any occasion. It’s easy to make and tastes divine with chocolate frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Whisk milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla in another.
  4. Combine mixtures and stir until smooth.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before frosting.
