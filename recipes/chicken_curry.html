Description

This flavorful chicken curry combines tender chicken pieces with a rich, spiced sauce that’s perfect with rice or chapati.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken pieces
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add curry powder and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and coconut milk, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season and serve hot with rice or chapati.
